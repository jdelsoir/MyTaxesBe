The opportunity

open data nobody can read

Belgium publishes 30,800+ open datasets. The federal budget is among them — fully open, free to reuse. But it ships as raw spreadsheets no citizen will ever open. No tool today lets an ordinary person see where their tax euro actually goes.

How it works

three moving parts

01

open data in

Official federal budget and tax-revenue files, pulled automatically from the government's own portals.

02

one build step

Converted to clean, ready-to-show data when the site is built. No servers, no database to run.

03

live for everyone

A fast, accessible website in three languages. Open it, pick your income, see your taxes.

the salary slider

Enter your income and watch the entire federal budget rewritten as your contribution — per day. Abstract billions become personal, in one gesture.
